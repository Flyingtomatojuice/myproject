INTRODUCTION

Logistics services are very important in today’s economy. It has a big role because it is primarily concerned with the movement of commodities. In the business world, logistics success leads to increased efficiency, reducing costs, higher production rates, better inventory control, wiser warehouse space utilization, improved customer and supplier satisfaction, and a better customer experience.

The logistics services business is described as the process of distributing resources and products in the appropriate quantities to the final user or destination. It performs a variety of functions, such as transportation and delivery, cargo handling, storage, packaging, distribution processing, and information processing. This service has been implemented to ensure that products are delivered quickly and on time from the location of the manufacturer or factory to the consumer. Most people are familiar with transportation services as a crucial component of logistics. This covers all means of transportation, including automobiles, freight trains, cargo shipping, and air travel.
